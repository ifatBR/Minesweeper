Instructions

The goal- reveal all cells and mark all mines.

Left click- reveals a cell.
Reight click- marks a cell so you can't click it.
You can click again to un-mark it.
Snowman- click the snowman to restart.

Cell types:
Empty- safe to click, first cell is always empty.
Number- safe to click. Indicates how many mine cells are around it (pependicular or diagonal).
Mine- left clikc- boom! you loose a life. Figure out where they are and mark them with right click.

Extra fitures:
Hint- press a '?' and a cell on the board to reveal it's area for 1 sec.
Lives- shows you how many lives you have left.
Undo- undo one step back.
Safe click- highlights a cell that is safe to click.
Manually place mines- Befor eyou start a game you can choose to place the mines yourself.
